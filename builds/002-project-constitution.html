Build #2 · Governing Document · v1.5.0

Project Constitution v1.0

The governing document for every future build decision on the Citizens United Education Platform.

1. Purpose

Create the most comprehensive public education resource ever built about Citizens United v. FEC. Educate—not merely inform. Education is the primary mission. Leadership development is the ultimate outcome.

2. Vision

An informed public is essential to a healthy representative democracy. Every citizen deserves clear, accurate, evidence-based explanations to reach their own informed conclusions.

3. Guiding Philosophy

People learn in layers. Never force overwhelming detail before basics. Learning is self-directed. Curiosity determines depth.

4. Core Reader Journey

Discover Understand Explore Evaluate Discuss Teach Lead

5. Project Principles

  • Education before advocacy
  • Facts distinguished from interpretation
  • Primary sources whenever possible
  • Plain language before legal terminology
  • Thoughtful civic participation
  • Respect differing viewpoints; ground in evidence
  • Continuous expansion as developments emerge

6. Civic Learning Model

  • Level One — One-minute overview
  • Level Two — Five-minute explanation
  • Level Three — Comprehensive article
  • Level Four — Research archive

7. Civic Action Philosophy

Education is the beginning—not the endpoint. Voluntary participation: network, leadership, conversations, research, legislation, ballot concepts, sharing. Empower—not pressure.

8. Persistent Action Hub

Floating hub on every page. See Civic Action docs and click Take Action on any page.

9. Leadership Development

Pathways: community educator, research volunteer, writer, data researcher, speaker, event host, campus organizer, faith/civic outreach, legislative research, ballot researcher. Every pathway begins with education.

10. Governance Test

Every feature must support at least one objective:

  1. Increase public understanding.
  2. Deepen evidence-based learning.
  3. Develop community education leaders.
