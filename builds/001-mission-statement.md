## Citizens United Education Website

This website exists to help ordinary Americans develop a deep, clear, evidence-based understanding of *Citizens United v. Federal Election Commission*.

Its purpose is not simply to tell people that *Citizens United* was important. Its purpose is to show them why it was important, what came before it, what the Supreme Court actually decided, how political spending changed afterward, and why the decision still shapes American democracy today.

The site will be built as a layered civic education system.

A first-time visitor should be able to understand the basic issue in five minutes. A serious reader should be able to keep drilling down through legal history, spending data, Supreme Court doctrine, reform strategies, current ballot initiatives, and original source materials.

Every major topic will move from plain-language explanation to deeper research.

The ultimate goal is to turn understanding into leadership.

The website will culminate in a signup pathway for people who want to help educate their own community about *Citizens United*, money in politics, and the ongoing effort to restore public power in American democracy.

---

## Core Mission

Build the most accessible, layered, and deeply sourced public education platform focused specifically on *Citizens United* and its consequences.

## Core Outcome

Move readers through this journey:

**Understand → Trust → Care → Teach → Lead**

## Primary Conversion Goal

Recruit people willing to become local education leaders in their city, county, campus, civic group, church, or community network.

## Guiding Principle

The site must never overwhelm the reader at the front door.

It should start simple, then let people go as deep as they want.
