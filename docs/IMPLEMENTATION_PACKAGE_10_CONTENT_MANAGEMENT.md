# Editorial Workflow

Every publication follows the same lifecycle.

```text
Draft
  ↓
AI Review
  ↓
Editorial Review
  ↓
Evidence Verification
  ↓
Accessibility Review
  ↓
Legal/Policy Review (when appropriate)
  ↓
Approval
  ↓
Publication
  ↓
Mission Control Indexing
  ↓
Periodic Review
```

No important publication skips the workflow.
