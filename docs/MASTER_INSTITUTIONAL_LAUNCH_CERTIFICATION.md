# The Twelve Certification Domains

1. **Research** — library, evidence, sources, editorial review, Claims Registry  
2. **Technology** — website, mobile, search, accounts, AI, security, backups  
3. **Mission Control** — executive/county/city/neighborhood/PMO dashboards, reporting, Digital Twin  
4. **Community Education Academy** — curriculum, pathways, certification, leader training  
5. **Volunteer Network** — onboarding, training, roles, leadership pathways, mentorship  
6. **Coalition Network** — onboarding, directory, resources, agreements, communications  
7. **Arkansas Coverage** — 75 counties, 250 cities, neighborhoods, leaders, conversations  
8. **Governance** — constitution, operating manual, PMO, decisions, policies, leadership  
9. **AI & LocalBrains** — LocalBrains operational, knowledge connected, assistants, Knowledge Platform  
10. **Communications** — website, social, video, press, email, presentations  
11. **Trust & Transparency** — citations, corrections, accessibility, privacy, annual review  
12. **Institutional Sustainability** — documentation, knowledge, succession, volunteers, finance, long-term plan

Each domain: Not Started → In Progress → Ready → Certified
