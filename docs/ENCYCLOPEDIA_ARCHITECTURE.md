# Citizens United Facts Research Encyclopedia & Knowledge Library

**Encyclopedia Architecture v1.0**  
**Dashboard:** [/mission-control/encyclopedia.html](/mission-control/encyclopedia.html) · **Registry:** [/data/encyclopedia-knowledge-library.json](/data/encyclopedia-knowledge-library.json)

---

# Purpose

The Citizens United Facts platform should become the definitive educational reference on *Citizens United*.

Rather than functioning as a traditional website, it should operate as a living encyclopedia where every topic, person, organization, court case, law, constitutional principle, historical event, dataset, chart, and reform proposal is connected through a single knowledge system.

This encyclopedia is the permanent institutional memory of the platform.

Mission Control should track the completeness of the encyclopedia over time.

---

# Encyclopedia Philosophy

Every page should answer one question exceptionally well.

Instead of creating long pages that attempt to explain everything, the encyclopedia should create thousands of interconnected educational entries that naturally guide readers toward deeper understanding.

Readers should always be able to answer:

* What is this?
* Why does it matter?
* How is it connected?
* What happened before?
* What happened after?
* Where can I verify it?
* What should I read next?

# Encyclopedia Entry Structure

Every entry should follow the same structure.

1. Plain-language summary
2. Why it matters
3. Historical context
4. Detailed explanation
5. Related encyclopedia entries
6. Primary sources
7. Frequently asked questions
8. Timeline placement
9. Suggested learning path

This consistency makes the encyclopedia easy to navigate.

# Citation Standards

Every encyclopedia entry should:

* Cite authoritative sources.
* Identify publication and review dates.
* Link to primary documents whenever available.
* Distinguish established facts from interpretation.
* Explain uncertainty when it exists.

Readers should be able to independently verify important claims.

---

# Future Expansion

The encyclopedia architecture should support future civic education topics without changing its structure.

If the organization later expands beyond *Citizens United*, the same encyclopedia framework can accommodate additional subjects while preserving consistent navigation, editorial standards, and Mission Control reporting.

---

# Governing Principle

The Citizens United Facts Encyclopedia should become Arkansas's most comprehensive educational reference on *Citizens United*.

Its purpose is not simply to answer questions.

Its purpose is to help every reader understand how the history, law, Constitution, political spending, reform proposals, and civic education efforts fit together into one coherent story supported by transparent evidence.
