# Purpose

**Build #78** creates the **Arkansas City Operating System (ArCOS)** — a repeatable framework for civic education teams in the **250 largest Arkansas cities**.

Cities are where most community conversations happen. Mission Control should treat cities as the **operational bridge** between county leadership and neighborhood relationships.

Integrates [Arkansas County Operating System (#77)](/mission-control/arkansas-county-operating-system.html), [Neighborhood Organizing (#57)](/mission-control/neighborhood-organizing.html), [Arkansas Command Strategy (#70)](/mission-control/arkansas-command-strategy.html), and [Civic Atlas (#58)](/mission-control/civic-atlas.html).

---

# Institutional Philosophy

Every city should eventually answer:

- Who are our Education Leaders?
- What organizations are helping educate the community?
- Where are conversations taking place?
- What educational events are scheduled?
- What questions are local residents asking?
- What progress have we made this year?

Every city becomes an **active learning community**.
