# Purpose

**Build #80** answers the final strategic question:

> *"If everything succeeds, what does Citizens United Facts look like twenty years from now?"*

Rather than planning for launch, this roadmap plans for **legacy**.

Mission Control should continually compare current state to this long-term vision.

Integrates [Organizational Constitution (#76)](/mission-control/organizational-constitution.html), [Institutional Roadmap V1–V10 (#44)](/mission-control/institutional-roadmap.html), [ACOS (#77)](/mission-control/arkansas-county-operating-system.html), [ArCOS (#78)](/mission-control/arkansas-city-operating-system.html), [ANOS (#79)](/mission-control/arkansas-neighborhood-operating-system.html), and [Master Plan (#55)](/mission-control/master-plan.html).

# Founding Vision

Help Arkansans understand *Citizens United* and strengthen constitutional literacy, civic participation, and public trust.

**The website is the beginning. The institution is the destination.**

# Measure of Success

**Not judged by:** traffic · press · awards · technology

**Judged by:** constitutional understanding · stronger communities · respectful dialogue · trusted citizen educators · better civic resources for future generations

---

# Governing Principle

> **Institutions are remembered not because they were launched. They are remembered because they endured.**

Designed to outlive founders, first volunteers, original technology, and earliest challenges.
