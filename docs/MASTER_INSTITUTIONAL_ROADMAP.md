# Purpose

This document defines how Citizens United Facts evolves over time.

It is not merely a software roadmap.

It is the roadmap for building an enduring civic education institution.

The objective is to ensure that every new feature, every research effort, every coalition partnership, and every educational program contributes to a coherent long-term vision.

Mission Control should use this roadmap to measure institutional maturity rather than simply software completion.

---

# Institutional Vision

The long-term aspiration is for Citizens United Facts to become:

* Arkansas's definitive educational resource on *Citizens United*.
* A trusted public archive of campaign finance history.
* A statewide network of civic educators.
* A research institution grounded in transparent evidence.
* A model for how complex constitutional issues can be explained to the public.

Technology serves that mission.

The institution comes first.

---

# Version 1 — Foundation

**Primary Goal:** Launch a trustworthy educational platform.

**Major Deliverables:** Public website, learning paths, Source Library, Mission Control, Coalition sign-on, Education Leader signup, Contact Network, Research Library, Community Education Academy.

**Success Measures:** Platform operational, core content published, first Education Leaders enrolled, first coalition organizations participating.

---

# Version 2 — Arkansas Expansion

**Primary Goal:** Establish statewide educational presence.

**Major Deliverables:** County pages for all 75 counties, regional outreach tools, coalition growth, community conversations, event calendar, presentation toolkit expansion.

**Success Measures:** Statewide county coverage, active Education Leaders across multiple regions, growing coalition participation.

---

# Version 3 — Research Institution

**Primary Goal:** Strengthen scholarship and transparency.

**Major Deliverables:** Expanded encyclopedia, Evidence Ledger growth, Claims Registry, additional primary sources, comparative state studies, enhanced Research Observatory.

**Success Measures:** Research completeness, citation quality, public trust indicators.

---

# Version 4 — Interactive Learning

**Primary Goal:** Transform learning through exploration.

**Major Deliverables:** Knowledge Graph, interactive laboratories, visual data explorer, constitutional explorer, timeline explorer, guided learning experiences.

**Success Measures:** Learning engagement, curriculum completion, educational effectiveness.

---

# Version 5 — Community Education

**Primary Goal:** Build Arkansas's civic education network.

**Major Deliverables:** Expanded Community Education Academy, County Education Teams, coalition development, community conversation program, volunteer coordination.

**Success Measures:** Community events, Education Leaders, coalition partnerships, county participation.

---

# Version 6 — Multimedia Institution

**Primary Goal:** Develop a comprehensive educational media library.

**Major Deliverables:** Documentary series, video library, audio lessons, interactive presentations, downloadable teaching resources.

**Success Measures:** Media usage, classroom adoption, community presentation support.

---

# Version 7 — Institutional Intelligence

**Primary Goal:** Strengthen knowledge management.

**Major Deliverables:** AI educational guide, intelligent search, personalized learning recommendations, knowledge gap detection, Mission Control intelligence.

**Success Measures:** Improved navigation, faster discovery, research efficiency.

---

# Version 8 — Educational Partnerships

**Primary Goal:** Expand collaboration throughout Arkansas.

**Major Deliverables:** Library partnerships, educational institution partnerships, civic organization partnerships, professional association partnerships, expanded coalition resources.

**Success Measures:** Partnership growth, resource adoption, community reach.

---

# Version 9 — Institutional Excellence

**Primary Goal:** Refine every system.

**Major Deliverables:** Accessibility improvements, editorial refinements, technical modernization, research modernization, archive expansion.

**Success Measures:** Platform quality, accessibility compliance, user satisfaction, institutional sustainability.

---

# Version 10 — Permanent Civic Institution

**Primary Goal:** Operate as a mature public civic education institution.

**Major Deliverables:** Fully integrated Knowledge Graph, comprehensive Research Library, complete curriculum, statewide Education Network, mature Mission Control, long-term governance framework.

**Success Measures:** Public trust, educational impact, research quality, coalition stability, institutional continuity.

# Cross-Version Priorities

Every version should continue improving: Research, Education, Technology, Accessibility, Transparency, Coalition development, Community education, Mission Control.

No area should become stagnant.

---

# Annual Strategic Review

Each year the institution should evaluate: educational progress, research quality, coalition development, county participation, technology health, user feedback, emerging legal developments.

Mission Control should support this annual review process.

---

# Preservation Strategy

The institution should preserve: research history, build history, editorial history, major decisions, version milestones, educational resources, source archives.

Institutional memory is an asset.

---

# Long-Term Sustainability

Planning should consider: leadership succession, documentation, editorial governance, technology maintenance, research continuity, community partnerships.

A durable institution depends on more than software.

---

# Success Definition

The platform succeeds when an Arkansan can begin with little or no background knowledge, explore reliable evidence at their own pace, understand the historical and constitutional context of *Citizens United*, discover well-documented perspectives on ongoing debates, and find opportunities to participate in civic education within their own community.

The platform's lasting contribution is not measured by its size, but by the quality of understanding it helps create.

---

# Governing Principle

Citizens United Facts is being built to outlast election cycles, technology trends, and individual contributors.

Every version should strengthen the institution's ability to preserve knowledge, expand civic education, maintain transparent research practices, and serve Arkansas as a trusted public resource for understanding one of the most consequential campaign finance decisions in modern American history.
