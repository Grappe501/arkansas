# Coalition Building & Public Outreach

**Build #13** · **Arkansas pilot** · **Dashboard:** [/mission-control/coalition.html](/mission-control/coalition.html)

---

## Purpose

The Coalition Building & Public Outreach System unites organizations across Arkansas around a shared commitment to educating the public about *Citizens United*.

Organizations are **not** joining a political campaign. They join an **educational coalition** dedicated to improving public understanding through research, civic education, and respectful dialogue.

---

## Platform Pillars

The homepage offers three parallel entry points:

| Path | Audience | Route |
|------|----------|-------|
| **I'm Here to Learn** | Individual visitors | [/explore/](/explore/) |
| **I Want to Help** | Volunteers & community educators | [/educate/](/educate/) |
| **My Organization Wants to Partner** | Nonprofits, civic groups, faith communities, schools | [/coalition/](/coalition/) |

These align with **Education**, **Leadership**, and **Coalition Building**.

---

## Coalition Directory

Organizations create a profile at [/coalition/join.html](/coalition/join.html).

**Eligible organization types:** nonprofits, civic groups, community organizations, neighborhood associations, veteran groups, faith communities, educational institutions, student organizations, business and professional associations, public policy and good-government organizations.

Each organization receives a public profile highlighting educational involvement. Organizations choose which fields are public or private.

**Data:** `data/coalition-directory.json` · **Schema:** `data/organization-profile-schema.json`

## Membership Levels

| Level | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Supporter** | Supports the educational mission |
| **Educational Partner** | Shares platform resources with members |
| **Community Partner** | Hosts educational events |
| **Research Partner** | Contributes research or expertise |
| **Strategic Partner** | Collaborates on statewide educational initiatives |

## Governing Principle

Success is measured not only by individual learners, but by the number of **trusted Arkansas organizations** that help educate their communities with accurate, well-sourced information about *Citizens United*.
