# Purpose

**Build #77** creates the **Arkansas County Operating System (ACOS)** — a standardized framework for building civic education in every county while preserving local identity.

The strength of Citizens United Facts will ultimately be measured **one county at a time**.

Mission Control should use ACOS as the **primary operational framework for statewide expansion**.

Extends [County Operating System (#31)](/mission-control/county-os.html); integrates [Organizational Constitution (#76)](/mission-control/organizational-constitution.html), [Arkansas Command Strategy (#70)](/mission-control/arkansas-command-strategy.html), [Civic Atlas (#58)](/mission-control/civic-atlas.html), and [Citizen Leadership Academy (#68)](/mission-control/citizen-leadership-academy.html).

---

# Institutional Philosophy

Every county should eventually answer:

- Who are our Education Leaders?
- Which organizations are involved?
- Where are community conversations happening?
- What educational resources are available?
- What questions are people asking?
- What progress have we made?
- What do we need next?

Every county becomes a **living educational system**.
