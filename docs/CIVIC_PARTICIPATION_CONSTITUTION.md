## Purpose

The platform **educates first**. Its second purpose is to build a **statewide network of informed Arkansans** who voluntarily educate their families, neighbors, civic organizations, and local communities.

**Version 1 is designed around Arkansas.** Every feature strengthens civic education within Arkansas while keeping architecture modular for future expansion.

---

## Arkansas Education Ladder (7 levels)

| Level | Role | Mission |
|-------|------|---------|
| **1** | Visitor | Curiosity and foundational understanding |
| **2** | Subscriber | Join Arkansas Education Network; stay connected |
| **3** | Community Supporter | Share through trusted relationships |
| **4** | Community Educator | Host evidence-based conversations |
| **5** | County Education Leader | Coordinate county-level education |
| **6** | Arkansas Research Contributor | Keep platform accurate and current |
| **7** | Arkansas Education Council | Sustain and improve the statewide network |

Mission Control tracks participation **by Arkansas county**.
