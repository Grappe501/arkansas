**Multi-Year Strategic Growth Blueprint v1.0**  
**Dashboard:** [/mission-control/campaign-os.html](/mission-control/campaign-os.html) · **Registry:** [/data/educational-campaign-operating-system.json](/data/educational-campaign-operating-system.json)

# Purpose

The Citizens United Facts platform is not intended to be a one-time educational website.

It is designed to become a long-term civic education institution that grows through deliberate, measurable phases.

This blueprint establishes the strategic roadmap from launch through statewide educational maturity.

Mission Control will use this document as the master roadmap for long-term planning.

---

# The Four Strategic Horizons

The platform grows through four horizons.

Each horizon builds upon the previous one.

No horizon is considered complete until its educational objectives have been achieved.

---

# Horizon One

## Build the Institution

Mission

Create Arkansas's most trusted educational resource on *Citizens United*.

Objectives

* Complete the educational platform.
* Build Mission Control.
* Publish the research library.
* Launch the Source Library.
* Launch coalition sign-on.
* Launch Education Leader program.
* Launch Community Education Academy.
* Build the first Arkansas county pages.

Primary Success Indicators

* Educational platform launched.
* Research framework operational.
* Mission Control operational.
* First coalition organizations participating.
* First Education Leaders enrolled.

---

# Risk Management

Mission Control should monitor strategic risks.

Examples:

* Outdated research.
* Incomplete citations.
* Technical debt.
* Limited county participation.
* Coalition inactivity.
* Accessibility gaps.
* Resource limitations.

Early identification allows thoughtful planning.

---

# Success Measurements

The platform should evaluate itself through educational outcomes.

Possible measures include:

* Learning path completion.
* Community conversations hosted.
* Education Leaders developed.
* Coalition organizations participating.
* Counties with active educational activity.
* Research completeness.
* Public resource usage.
* Returning learners.

These indicators better reflect the mission than simple website traffic.

---

# Institutional Memory

Mission Control should preserve:

* Build history.
* Major decisions.
* Research milestones.
* Coalition milestones.
* Educational milestones.
* Annual reports.
* Strategic plans.

This creates continuity as the platform evolves over years.

---

# Future Readiness

The architecture should remain flexible enough to support future educational topics beyond *Citizens United* if the organization chooses to expand its mission.

The initial focus remains entirely on *Citizens United*, but the technical and organizational framework should allow additional civic education initiatives to be added without rebuilding the platform.

---

# Governing Principle

The Citizens United Facts platform is being built as an institution rather than a project.

Projects have end dates.

Institutions have missions.

Every decision should strengthen the platform's ability to educate Arkansans accurately, transparently, and consistently for many years to come.
