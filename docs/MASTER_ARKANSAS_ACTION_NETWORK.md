# Purpose

By Build #64, the institution has a research engine, knowledge engine, curriculum, coalition, county framework, neighborhood framework, and relationship operating system.

This build answers the most important operational question:

> **How do we intentionally grow from one visitor into a statewide network of 200,000 Arkansans committed to civic education?**

The **Arkansas Action Network** is a structured leadership and growth system that transforms learning into sustainable local capacity.

Mission Control treats this as the institution's **primary growth engine**.

**Build #64** — Unifies and operationalizes [Statewide Growth (#56)](/docs/MASTER_STATEWIDE_GROWTH.md), [Neighborhood Organizing (#57)](/docs/MASTER_NEIGHBORHOOD_ORGANIZING.md), [Civic Atlas (#58)](/docs/MASTER_CIVIC_ATLAS.md), [Relationship OS (#59)](/docs/MASTER_RELATIONSHIP_OS.md), [Coalition Network (#61)](/docs/MASTER_COALITION_NETWORK.md), and [Citizen Action Center (#62)](/docs/MASTER_CITIZEN_ACTION_CENTER.md).

# Mission

Build permanent civic education infrastructure across Arkansas by developing local leaders, supporting trusted relationships, and strengthening communities through evidence-based learning.

**Growth is measured in people equipped to teach others — not simply website traffic.**

# The Arkansas Leadership Pyramid

| Level | Role | Goal | MC Tracks |
|-------|------|------|-----------|
| 1 | **Visitor** | Learn | First visit, topics explored, learning interests |
| 2 | **Subscriber** | Stay connected | Newsletter, preferences, county, city |
| 3 | **Active Learner** | Complete learning paths | Curriculum, saved resources, videos, research |
| 4 | **Community Participant** | Participate locally | Conversations, volunteer, share resources |
| 5 | **Neighborhood Education Leader** | Educate neighbors | Host conversations, share materials, recruit |
| 6 | **City Education Leader** | Coordinate city efforts | Support neighborhood leaders, events, partnerships |
| 7 | **County Education Director** | County-wide coordination | Recruit, mentor, coalition, expand reach |
| 8 | **Regional Mentor** | Strengthen multiple counties | Mentor county leaders, best practices, expansion |

# Long-Term Vision

Fifteen years after launch: every county has an active education team; every major city has trained leaders; neighborhood discussions occur regularly; coalition organizations collaborate; thousands of Education Leaders mentor new volunteers; **200,000 Arkansans** share reliable civic education through trusted relationships.

The institution grows organically because local leaders continuously develop new local leaders.

---

# Governing Principle

The most effective way to strengthen civic understanding is to **equip ordinary Arkansans to teach other ordinary Arkansans**.

One conversation becomes ten. Ten become one hundred. One hundred become a community. Communities become counties. Counties become a statewide movement grounded in evidence, transparency, respectful dialogue, and constitutional self-government.
