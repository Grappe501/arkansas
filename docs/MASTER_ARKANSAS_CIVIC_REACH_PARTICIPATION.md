# Purpose

**Build #86** establishes the **15% Connected Citizen Goal** — voluntary relationships with at least 15% of registered voters in every Arkansas county and major city.

Participation means staying connected for educational updates — **not** agreement.

Mission Control makes this a primary statewide performance indicator.

---

# Civic Reach Score

**Active Connected Participants ÷ Registered Voters = Civic Reach Percentage**

Every county and city maintains a Civic Reach Score, continuously updated in Mission Control.
