# Purpose

**Build #96** — the platform should feel like an **operating system for civic education in Arkansas**, not a traditional website.

Every account enters a personalized civic workspace. The platform remembers learning, interests, location, organizations, volunteer roles, and goals.

> **One login. One platform. Everything connected.**

# Institutional Philosophy

Every Arkansan should feel: **"This platform was built for me."**

The system adapts to the individual while remaining part of one statewide institution.

# Role-Based Workspaces

**Volunteers** — projects, responsibilities, mentorship, training, leadership pathway, team comms, resource library.

**Education Leaders** — presentation resources, conversation kits, county/city dashboards, volunteer roster, events, local AI, MC recommendations.

**Coalition partners** — organization profile, resources, shared calendar, volunteer opportunities, events, partnership tracking.

**Executives** — Mission Control, Digital Twin, PMO, department dashboards, strategic planning, institution health, statewide maps.

---

# AI Personal Assistant · Smart Notifications · Mission Control Integration

AI understands role, learning, assignments, calendar, projects, county, organizations, institutional knowledge.

Notifications: research, county events, conversations, legislative education, academy reminders, volunteer assignments, deadlines.

MC views anonymous aggregate trends — learning, volunteer engagement, county/city/neighborhood participation, coalition activity, platform usage.

---

# Security & Privacy

User controls: communication preferences, notifications, profile visibility, volunteer visibility, organization memberships, personal information.
