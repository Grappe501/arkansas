# Purpose

**Build #84** establishes the **long-range strategic plan** — what the institution will accomplish over the next decade.

Unlike the [Master Build Plan](/BUILD_PLAN.md) (constructing the institution), this Strategic Plan focuses on **accomplishment** through 2035.

**January 2027** is the **V1 mission-complete milestone** within this decade plan — see [Execution Schedule (#88)](/mission-control/execution-schedule.html).

Used by Mission Control, leadership, Education Leaders, coalition partners, and volunteers.

Integrates [Civic Ecosystem (#83)](/mission-control/arkansas-civic-ecosystem.html), [Legacy Roadmap (#80)](/mission-control/arkansas-civic-institution-roadmap.html), [Command Strategy (#70)](/mission-control/arkansas-command-strategy.html).

# Strategic Vision (2035)

Arkansas's most trusted institution for understanding:

- *Citizens United v. Federal Election Commission*
- Campaign finance · Constitutional government · Civic participation
- Government transparency · Public policy processes

Knowledge accessible to every Arkansan.

---

# Strategic Mission

Enduring statewide civic education network grounded in: Evidence, Research, Volunteer leadership, Community conversations, Constitutional literacy, Public trust.

---

# Seven Strategic Goals

| # | Goal | Focus |
|---|------|-------|
| 1 | Build the Institution | MC, Knowledge Graph, Academy, Observatory, Atlas, volunteers |
| 2 | Build the Knowledge Base | Citizens United, campaign finance, constitutional history, Arkansas civic processes |
| 3 | Build Leadership | Education Leaders, coordinators, directors, mentors, fellows |
| 4 | Build Communities | 75 counties · 250 cities · neighborhoods statewide |
| 5 | Build Partnerships | Libraries, colleges, historical societies, nonprofits, civic orgs |
| 6 | Build Trust | Transparent research, verification, accountability, accessibility |
| 7 | Build Legacy | Succession, documentation, memory, stewardship, modernization |

# Milestones

**Five-year:** Operational MC, comprehensive curriculum, county leadership, growing coalition, Research Institute, statewide conversations.

**Ten-year:** Education Leaders in all counties, city leadership teams, mature Academy, comprehensive library, strong reputation, neighborhood network, broad coalition.

---

# Governing Principle

> **Strategic planning is not about predicting the future. It is about preparing the institution to serve Arkansas regardless of what the future brings.**

Every year: more knowledgeable, trusted, connected, resilient, useful, capable, worthy of confidence.
