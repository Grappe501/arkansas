# Purpose

Most websites measure success by page views.

Citizens United Facts should measure success by transformation.

The platform should answer one question:

> **"How does someone who has never heard of Citizens United become a trusted community educator?"**

This document designs that transformation — the human journey through the institution.

Mission Control should track every stage of that journey.

Extends Build #8 Citizen Journey Blueprint (`/data/ux-journey.json`) — 7-stage ladder evolves to 8-stage transformation model.

---

# The Educational Transformation Model

Every visitor moves through eight stages. Not everyone reaches the final stage. But every page should help someone move one step forward.

**Curiosity** → **Discovery** → **Understanding** → **Confidence** → **Connection** → **Participation** → **Leadership** → **Legacy**

---

# Stage One — Curiosity

The visitor knows very little. Platform goal: reduce intimidation. Answer "What is this?" Success metric: visitor begins learning.

---

# Stage Two — Discovery

The visitor explores — articles, timelines, videos, glossary, interactive tools. Platform goal: keep curiosity alive. Success metric: multiple lessons completed.

---

# Stage Three — Understanding

History → Law → Constitution → Money → Politics → Arkansas. The subject becomes coherent. Success metric: learning path completion.

---

# Stage Four — Confidence

The visitor feels capable of explaining what Citizens United is, why it matters, what changed, and what people disagree about. Success metric: downloads teaching materials.

---

# Stage Five — Connection

Joins Contact Network, attends events, joins coalition, asks questions, downloads presentations. Success metric: first meaningful participation.

---

# Stage Six — Participation

Hosts discussions, shares resources, invites friends, works with organizations, participates in Academy. Success metric: educational activity recorded.

---

# Stage Seven — Leadership

Becomes an Education Leader — teaching, mentoring, research, presentations, coalition work, community conversations. Success metric: leadership profile established.

---

# Stage Eight — Legacy

Mentors new leaders, improves resources, contributes research, builds county education, strengthens coalition. Success metric: long-term contributions.

---

# Journey Map

Visitors → Learners → Returning Learners → Participants → Education Leaders → Coalition Members → County Leaders → Mentors

---

# Decision Points

Gentle invitations throughout: Continue Learning → View Sources → Watch Documentary → Explore Timeline → Join Contact Network → Teach Others → Join Coalition → Lead Community Conversation

Never pressure participation. Each invitation should feel like the natural next step.

---

# Behavioral Design Principles

Encourage: curiosity, reflection, critical thinking, evidence exploration, respectful discussion, continued learning, community engagement.

Not outrage. Not urgency. Not fear. Understanding comes first.

---

# Motivation System

Celebrate meaningful educational milestones: first lesson, first learning path, toolkit download, first community conversation, coalition join, research contribution, mentoring another Education Leader.

Recognition emphasizes learning and service — not competition.

---

# Journey Analytics

Mission Control monitors: average learning depth, lesson completion, return rate, Academy enrollment, leadership development, coalition participation, county participation, presentation activity, community conversations.

These measure educational transformation — not simple traffic.

---

# Institutional Philosophy

The platform should never ask: "How do we get more clicks?"

Instead: "How do we help one more Arkansan move one step deeper into understanding?"

---

# Governing Principle

Citizens United Facts exists to transform curiosity into civic understanding.

Success is measured not by how many people visit — but by how many people leave better equipped to help others understand one of the most important constitutional and campaign finance decisions of the modern era.
