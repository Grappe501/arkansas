# Purpose

The platform exists to educate. Education leads many visitors to ask:

> **"Now that I understand this better, how can I participate constructively?"**

The **Citizen Action Center** provides structured, educational pathways for civic participation.

Its role is not to tell people what position to take. It helps Arkansans participate **thoughtfully, respectfully, and effectively** using reliable information.

Mission Control views the Citizen Action Center as the **bridge between learning and community participation**.

**Build #62** — Integrates [Civic Action Lab (#42)](/mission-control/civic-action-lab.html), [Coalition Network (#61)](/docs/MASTER_COALITION_NETWORK.md), [Visitor Journey (#47)](/mission-control/visitor-journey.html), and [Statewide Growth (#56)](/docs/MASTER_STATEWIDE_GROWTH.md).

# Guiding Philosophy

**Learn → Understand → Verify → Discuss → Participate**

Education always comes before action.

---

# The Six Citizen Pathways

| # | Pathway | Focus |
|---|---------|-------|
| 1 | **Stay Informed** | Continue learning — updates, research, resources, events |
| 2 | **Share Knowledge** | Help others learn — fact sheets, QR codes, guides, graphics |
| 3 | **Become an Education Leader** | Academy training, presentations, conversations, mentoring |
| 4 | **Strengthen the Coalition** | Join coalition, host events, share resources |
| 5 | **Civic Participation Resources** | How government processes work — without advocacy scripts |
| 6 | **Research & Resource Contributions** | Submit sources, corrections, stories — editorial review |

Every visitor chooses the level that fits their interests and availability.
