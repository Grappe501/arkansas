# Purpose

**Build #85** defines what must exist **by January 2027** for Citizens United Facts to launch as a credible, trusted, statewide civic education institution.

This is the **operational checklist for launch** — not a wish list.

Mission Control uses this as the official **Launch Readiness Blueprint**.

Distinct from [Launch Strategy (#53)](/mission-control/launch-strategy.html) (rollout phases) and [Strategic Plan 2035 (#84)](/mission-control/arkansas-strategic-plan-2035.html) (decade goals).

---

# Launch Mission

**January 2027** — Arkansas's premier civic education platform on Citizens United, campaign finance, constitutional government, civic participation, transparency, ballot initiatives, and the legislative process.

Built by Arkansans for Arkansans.
