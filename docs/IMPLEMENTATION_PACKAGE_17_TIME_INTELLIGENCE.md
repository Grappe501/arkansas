# Purpose

Time is one of the institution's most valuable resources.

The **Time Intelligence System** coordinates every meeting, class, community conversation, volunteer assignment, leadership event, publication schedule, project milestone, and organizational deadline across Citizens United Facts.

Every LocalBrain owns its own calendar. Mission Control understands them all.

---

# Time Philosophy

Nothing important should be forgotten. Nothing should be scheduled in isolation.

Every event should have: Purpose · Participants · Preparation · Resources · Follow-up

Mission Control should always understand what is happening today, tomorrow, next month, and next year.
