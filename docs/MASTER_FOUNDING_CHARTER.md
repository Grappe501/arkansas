# Preamble

A free people cannot govern themselves if they cannot understand the systems that govern them.

Knowledge is the foundation of liberty. Understanding is the foundation of participation. Trust is the foundation of healthy institutions.

---

# Why We Exist · What We Are Building · Who We Serve

Civic questions belong to the people — not only lawyers, academics, lobbyists, or elected officials.

We are building an institution, not a campaign. A public educational resource, volunteer network, research center, leadership academy, coalition, and permanent civic education infrastructure.

---

# Our Commitments · Our Standard · Our Responsibility

Research before assumption. Evidence before opinion. Transparency before convenience.

Every page honest. Every citation verifiable. Every volunteer valued. Every correction made openly.

Every generation becomes a steward.

---

# Our Goals · January 2027

January 2027 marks the completion of the founding build — not the completion of the mission.

Statewide civic education institution. Mission Control. Education Leaders. 75 counties. 250 cities. Academy. Coalition. Research library. Volunteer-powered organization.

---

# The Next Generation · Our Measure of Success · The Arkansas Promise

Success measured by understanding, stronger communities, volunteer leaders, neighbors learning together, increased trust, and faithfulness to mission.

Arkansas can become a national example — volunteer by volunteer, county by county, generation by generation.

---

# Founder's Closing Reflection · Institutional Covenant

We will seek truth with humility. Teach with integrity. Serve without expectation of recognition. Protect the public trust. Leave the institution stronger for those who follow.

---

# Final Governing Principle

**Knowledge strengthens citizens.**

**Citizens strengthen communities.**

**Communities strengthen Arkansas.**

**And Arkansas, strengthened by informed citizens, strengthens the American promise of self-government.**

**This is the founding charter of Citizens United Facts.**
