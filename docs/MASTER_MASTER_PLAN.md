# The Why

One Supreme Court decision changed the national conversation about campaign finance, political spending, and the First Amendment.

Millions have heard "Citizens United." Very few can accurately explain what the case involved, what the Court held, what changed, what did not change, how law evolved, or what options exist today.

The institution exists to close that educational gap **for Arkansas**.

---

# The Mission

Build Arkansas's most trusted, transparent, evidence-based educational institution devoted to understanding **Citizens United v. Federal Election Commission**.

The institution exists to educate. Everything else supports education.

---

# The Vision
