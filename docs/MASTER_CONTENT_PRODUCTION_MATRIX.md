# Purpose

Up to this point, we have designed the institution.

Now we design **everything the institution must eventually contain**.

Every page. Every chart. Every timeline. Every video. Every lesson. Every infographic. Every research paper. Every downloadable PDF. Every presentation. Every educational packet.

Everything receives an ID and enters the production queue.

# Production Status

Every asset moves through the same production stages:

**Not Started** → **Research** → **Outline** → **Writing** → **Fact Review** → **Citation Review** → **Visual Design** → **Editorial Review** → **Accessibility Review** → **Published** → **Scheduled Review**

Mission Control displays the status of every asset.

---

# Production IDs

Every asset receives a permanent identifier.

Examples: `ARTICLE-0147` · `CASE-0021` · `TIMELINE-0084` · `VIDEO-0039` · `INFOGRAPHIC-0018` · `LESSON-0054` · `SOURCE-0411` · `COUNTY-0035` · `MODELLAW-0012` · `CLAIM-0837`

Nothing is ever anonymous.

Extends Build #6 Content Inventory and Build #7 MRID traceability.

# Institutional Vision

Eventually, Citizens United Facts becomes one of the largest educational collections devoted to a single Supreme Court decision ever assembled — not because it contains the most pages, but because every page, lesson, source, chart, video, and educational experience is carefully connected, thoroughly researched, transparently documented, and designed to help Arkansans understand a complex constitutional issue with confidence.

# Governing Principle

The Content Production Matrix transforms vision into execution.

Every educational asset has a place. Every asset has a workflow. Every asset has a purpose.

Mission Control always knows what exists, what is being built, what remains unfinished, and what should come next.

The institution grows deliberately, one verified educational resource at a time.
