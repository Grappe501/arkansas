# Purpose

The Research Library is the heart of the Citizens United Facts platform.

Most websites summarize information.

This platform should preserve it.

Every important document, court opinion, statute, congressional hearing, academic paper, campaign finance report, historical record, dataset, timeline, and educational resource should become part of a carefully organized digital archive.

The goal is to build the largest publicly understandable knowledge repository on *Citizens United* and its historical context.

Mission Control should treat the Research Library as one of the institution's most valuable assets.

---

# Library Philosophy

The platform should function like a hybrid of:

* A presidential library.
* A university research library.
* A constitutional archive.
* A legal reference center.
* A civic education museum.

The collection should be organized for both casual readers and serious researchers.

# Long-Term Vision

The Research Library should become the definitive public archive supporting the Citizens United Facts platform.

A student, journalist, teacher, legislator, researcher, or interested citizen should be able to begin with a simple question and ultimately trace every important claim back to the underlying documents and evidence.

The library should preserve knowledge while making it understandable.

---

# Governing Principle

The Citizens United Facts Research Library exists to ensure that evidence is never hidden behind conclusions.

Its purpose is to organize, preserve, explain, and connect the documentary record so that every Arkansan has access to the historical and legal materials necessary to understand *Citizens United* for themselves.

The platform should not simply tell the story.

It should preserve the record from which the story is told.
