# County Readiness Certification

Every county passes a readiness review. Certification includes:

County dashboard operational · County profile complete · Education Leader identified or in training · Volunteer pipeline active · Community calendar established · Community conversations scheduled · County Academy operational · Mission Control reporting active · Research resources available · **At least one active organizational partner identified and participating**

The county becomes officially certified only after all criteria are satisfied.

# Organizational Partnership Certification

By **October 1, 2026**, each of Arkansas's **75 counties** should have documented partnerships with one or more local organizations that support civic education.

Potential partners may include:

Libraries · Historical societies · Community colleges · Universities · Civic organizations · Neighborhood associations · Educational nonprofits · Youth organizations · Community service organizations · Faith-based organizations engaged in civic education · Local nonprofit organizations

Mission Control monitors:

Partnership status · Partnership activity · Shared events · Shared educational resources · Joint community conversations · Partner onboarding progress

This milestone is one of the institution's highest operational priorities.
