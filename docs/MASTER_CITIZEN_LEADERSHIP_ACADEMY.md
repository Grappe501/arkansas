# Purpose

Knowledge alone does not build institutions. **People do.**

The **Citizen Leadership Academy** identifies, educates, mentors, certifies, and supports Arkansans who want to become trusted civic education leaders.

The Academy is the **human engine** of Citizens United Facts. Mission Control considers it one of the institution's **highest strategic priorities**.

**Build #68** — Extends and unifies [Community Education Academy (#28)](/mission-control/education-academy.html), [Arkansas Action Network (#64)](/docs/MASTER_ARKANSAS_ACTION_NETWORK.md), [Impact Measurement (#67)](/docs/MASTER_IMPACT_MEASUREMENT.md), and [Citizen Action Center (#62)](/docs/MASTER_CITIZEN_ACTION_CENTER.md).

---

# Academy Mission

Develop thousands of Arkansans who can confidently:

- Explain *Citizens United* accurately
- Teach constitutional concepts clearly
- Lead respectful community discussions
- Use evidence responsibly
- Build local educational networks
- Mentor future Education Leaders

The Academy produces **teachers, not spokespeople**.

---

# Academy Philosophy

**Learn → Practice → Teach → Lead → Mentor → Multiply**

Every graduate helps develop future leaders.

---

# Six Certification Levels

| Level | Title | Purpose | Outcome |
|-------|-------|---------|---------|
| 1 | **Civic Learner** | Foundational knowledge | Confident learner |
| 2 | **Community Ambassador** | Share educational resources | Trusted community resource |
| 3 | **Education Leader** | Teach small groups | Certified Education Leader |
| 4 | **City Education Coordinator** | Support city-wide education | City leadership certification |
| 5 | **County Education Director** | County-wide infrastructure | County education leadership |
| 6 | **Arkansas Civic Fellow** | Statewide institutional leaders | Statewide mentor and steward |
