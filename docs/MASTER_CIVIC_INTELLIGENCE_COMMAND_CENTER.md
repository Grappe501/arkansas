# Purpose

By Build #65, nearly every major institutional component has been designed. This build **unifies them into one executive command center**.

Mission Control is no longer simply a project dashboard. It becomes the **operational brain** of the entire institution.

Every department · every county · every city · every Education Leader · every coalition partner · every research project · every article · every relationship · every event — **everything reports into one living system**.

If someone asked:

> **"How healthy is Citizens United Facts today?"**

Mission Control should answer in **less than five seconds**.

**Build #65** — Unifies and extends [Mission Control 2.0 (#25)](/mission-control/executive.html), [Civic Atlas (#58)](/docs/MASTER_CIVIC_ATLAS.md), [Arkansas Action Network (#64)](/docs/MASTER_ARKANSAS_ACTION_NETWORK.md), [Institutional AI (#60)](/docs/MASTER_INSTITUTIONAL_AI.md), and all department dashboards into one executive nervous system.
