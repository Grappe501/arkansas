# Mission Control 2.0 & Executive Command Center

**Executive Operating System v1.0** · **Build #25**  
**Dashboard:** [/mission-control/executive.html](/mission-control/executive.html) · **Registry:** [/data/mc2-executive.json](/data/mc2-executive.json)

---

## Purpose

Mission Control is more than a project dashboard.

It is the operating system for the entire Citizens United Facts initiative.

Every build, every page, every source, every volunteer, every coalition partner, every educational event, every county, and every research project should ultimately report into Mission Control.

The goal is simple:

**If it matters, it is visible.**

---

## Executive Philosophy

Mission Control should answer the following questions within seconds:

* Where are we?
* What is complete?
* What needs attention?
* Where are we succeeding?
* Where are we falling behind?
* What should we build next?

Everything else is secondary.

---

## Executive Dashboard

The opening screen should provide a statewide snapshot.

### Primary panels

**Platform Completion** — Overall completion percentage.

**Current Build** — Active build number, current milestone, estimated completion.

**Active Development** — Features currently under construction.

**Public Readiness** — Educational, technical, research, coalition, and launch readiness.

**Arkansas Readiness Map** — Interactive county map showing education leaders, coalition organizations, community conversations, events, counties with no activity, and counties needing outreach. This should become one of the signature visualizations of the platform.

---

## Mission Control Navigation

Mission Control should contain specialized workspaces.

### Executive Overview

High-level project status.

### Development Center

Tracks phases, builds, components, routes, features, bugs, and enhancements.

### Research Center

Tracks sources collected, facts verified, pages needing citations, research gaps, and review schedule.

### Education Center

Tracks lessons completed, resources published, downloads, popular learning paths, FAQ growth, and educational toolkit usage.

### Coalition Center

Tracks organizations, counties represented, new sign-ons, partnership levels, and organization referrals.

### Community Education Center

Tracks events, community conversations, speakers, presentations, and resource requests.

### Public Official Education Center

Tracks educational outreach to Arkansas U.S. House members, U.S. Senators, and members of the Arkansas General Assembly.

Metrics include educational packets prepared, shared, resource requests, and follow-up reminders. Focus is educational engagement and relationship management.

### Communications Center

Tracks newsletter growth, website traffic, social media performance, referral activity, share rates, and returning visitors.

## Smart Alerts

Mission Control should identify areas needing attention:

* Counties without education leaders
* Pages needing citation review
* Coalition applications awaiting review
* Research nearing scheduled updates
* Broken links
* Incomplete learning paths
* Upcoming educational events
* Pending public official resource requests

Alerts should inform, not overwhelm.

## Success Dashboard

The platform should define success through educational impact.

**Education** — People completing learning paths, toolkit downloads, source library usage.

**Community** — Community conversations, counties with active education leaders, coalition organizations.

**Research** — Verified facts, sources cataloged, pages fully cited.

**Growth** — Returning visitors, volunteer signups, organization partnerships.

Mission Control should avoid measuring success solely through page views.

* Review queues

## Executive Principles

Mission Control should embody five principles:

**Visibility** — Nothing important is hidden.

**Transparency** — Progress and gaps are visible.

**Accountability** — Every major initiative has an owner and status.

**Evidence** — Metrics are grounded in verifiable information.

**Continuous Improvement** — Mission Control evolves alongside the platform.

---

## The Long-Term Vision

Mission Control should eventually become the single place where the entire Citizens United Facts initiative can be understood.

A project leader should be able to open one screen and immediately understand:

* The health of the educational platform
* The strength of the Arkansas coalition
* The reach of community education
* The status of research
* The progress of development
* The readiness for future growth

Mission Control is not simply an administrative dashboard.

It is the institutional memory, operational headquarters, and strategic planning center for the entire project.

---

## Governing Principle

The best Mission Control system does not merely report what has happened.

It helps the team understand what should happen next.

Every chart, every metric, every alert, and every report should ultimately support one goal:

**Helping more Arkansans understand Citizens United through accurate, transparent, evidence-based civic education.**
