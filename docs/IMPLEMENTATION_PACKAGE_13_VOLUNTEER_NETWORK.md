# Purpose

People—not software—will determine whether Citizens United Facts succeeds.

Software organizes. Mission Control coordinates. AI assists. **Volunteers build the institution.**

This package creates the complete volunteer operating system that recruits, trains, develops, supports, and retains thousands of Arkansans working together toward one shared educational mission.

---

# Volunteer Philosophy

Every volunteer should feel:

- "I know why I matter."
- "I know what I'm supposed to do."
- "I know who can help me."
- "I know what comes next."

The platform should remove uncertainty and make service rewarding.

# The Volunteer Journey

Every volunteer follows the same progression.

```text
Interested Citizen
  ↓
Registered Member
  ↓
Volunteer
  ↓
Trained Volunteer
  ↓
Education Leader
  ↓
County or City Leader
  ↓
Mentor
  ↓
Institutional Leader
```

Mission Control tracks every stage.
