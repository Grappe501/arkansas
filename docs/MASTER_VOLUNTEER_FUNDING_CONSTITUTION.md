# Purpose

Long-term credibility depends on research quality **and** the integrity of how the institution is organized and supported.

**Build #75** establishes founding principles protecting independence and public trust — prominently documented and reflected throughout the platform.

Mission Control monitors adherence alongside every other institutional health indicator.

Extends [Sustainability & Stewardship (#66)](/mission-control/sustainability-stewardship.html), [Governance Constitution (#49)](/mission-control/governance.html), and [Coalition Network (#61)](/mission-control/coalition-network.html).

# Founding Principle

Citizens United Facts is founded as an **all-volunteer Arkansas civic education institution**.

Strength comes from Arkansans who serve through civic education. **The institution exists to educate first and always.**

---

# Volunteer First

Every major function designed for trained volunteers: research · writing · conversations · Education Leaders · county leadership · coalition coordination · events · presentations · outreach · mentorship.

Mission Control celebrates volunteer service as one of the institution's greatest assets.

---

# Financial Independence · Arkansas Citizen Support

Operate with goal of remaining independent from interests that could call educational neutrality into question.

Preferred funding model when support is necessary: **individual voluntary contributions from Arkansas citizens.**

Coalition organizations contribute through collaboration, facilities, volunteers, expertise, programming, resources — **not conflated with financial support.**

---

# Transparency · No Special Access

Clear records of revenue, expenses, policies, governance. Public transparency consistent with law and public trust.

Contributions never purchase: editorial influence · research outcomes · conclusions · governance authority · preferred treatment.

---

# Stewardship Philosophy

Every dollar answers: **"How does this improve civic education for Arkansas?"**

Mission Control connects investments to mission outcomes.

---

# Volunteer Recognition · Sustainability · MC Dashboard

Recognize service milestones, leadership, mentorship, achievements, community impact — honors service not status.

Built to continue during limited financial resources: documentation · volunteer leadership · efficient technology · shared knowledge · careful planning.

Dashboard: active volunteers · hours · Education Leaders · county coverage · coalition participation · conversations · sustainability indicators.

---

# Governing Principle

Citizens United Facts belongs to the people who build it and the communities it serves.

Foundation: volunteer service. Mission: civic education. Strength: public trust.

Education remains independent, transparent, evidence-based, and rooted in voluntary support of Arkansans who believe an informed citizenry is essential to self-government.
