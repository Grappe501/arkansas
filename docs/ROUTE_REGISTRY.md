---

## Purpose

Every major route for Version 1 of the Arkansas-focused platform. Each route supports one of four purposes:

1. **Educate** the public
2. **Build trust** through evidence
3. **Move readers** into community education
4. **Track progress** through Mission Control

## Route Principle

Every route answers one clear reader question. If a page does not educate, build trust, support civic participation, or help manage the project, it should not exist in Version 1.
