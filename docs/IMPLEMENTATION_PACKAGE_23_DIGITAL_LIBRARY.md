# Purpose

Knowledge has little value if it cannot be found.

Citizens United Facts will eventually contain tens of thousands of documents, videos, images, research papers, presentations, meeting notes, legal authorities, educational resources, and historical records.

The **Document Management System (DMS)** becomes the institutional library for everything the organization creates, receives, or preserves.

Nothing important should ever be lost.

---

# Library Philosophy

Every institutional asset should be:

Stored once · Versioned · Searchable · Connected · Protected · Reusable · Archived

Every document should become part of the institution's permanent memory.
