## Purpose

The **ACEI Coalition** is the **statewide organizational backbone** of the Arkansas Civic Education Initiative's *Citizens United* education platform.

While the website educates individuals, the ACEI Coalition System connects organizations, institutions, and community leaders into a coordinated educational network. The coalition is not merely a list of supporters — it is a living ecosystem.

Mission Control treats **coalition growth as a primary measure of success**.

---

## Coalition Vision

At least **one active educational partner in every Arkansas county**. Each partner maintains organizational independence while contributing according to its mission, capacity, and expertise.

---

## Coalition Categories

Organizations self-identify within one or more categories:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Civic Organizations | Civic clubs, community associations, neighborhood organizations |
| Nonprofit Organizations | Civic engagement, education, transparency, public policy |
| Educational Institutions | Colleges, universities, student organizations |
| Faith Communities | Churches hosting educational discussions |
| Professional Organizations | Legal, business, professional societies |
| Veterans Organizations | Organizations serving veterans and military families |
| Community Service | Rotary, Kiwanis, Lions, local service organizations |

---

## Participation Levels

1. **Observer** — Learning about the coalition  
2. **Supporter** — Publicly supports the mission  
3. **Educational Partner** — Uses and distributes materials  
4. **Event Partner** — Hosts or co-hosts education events  
5. **Research Partner** — Contributes research and expertise  
6. **Strategic Partner** — Statewide educational collaboration

## Governing Principle

Success is measured by the coalition's ability to help more Arkansans understand *Citizens United*, engage with reliable information, and share knowledge in their communities. Every new partner expands the platform's capacity to educate Arkansas.
