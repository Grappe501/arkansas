---

## Purpose

Arkansas is not one audience.

It is 75 counties with different histories, demographics, economies, civic organizations, media markets, educational institutions, and community leaders.

The County Operating System transforms the platform from a statewide website into a locally relevant educational network.

Every county should eventually have its own educational presence while remaining connected to the statewide mission.

Mission Control should allow the team to understand the educational health of every county in Arkansas.

---

## County Philosophy

The platform grows one county at a time.

The goal is meaningful educational engagement within every Arkansas county.

Every county should eventually answer:

* Who is learning?
* Who is teaching?
* Which organizations are participating?
* What events are occurring?
* Which public officials represent this county?
* What educational gaps remain?

## County Profile

Every county receives a dedicated profile at `/arkansas/[county-name]`.

Each profile includes:

* County overview
* Population snapshot
* Legislative districts
* Congressional district
* State Senate districts
* State House districts
* Major communities
* Coalition partners
* Education Leaders
* Upcoming educational events
* Community conversations
* Available resources

The county page becomes the local home for civic education.

## County Dashboard

Mission Control calculates a County Education Score based on objective measures.

### Learning

Registered learners · Returning learners · Learning path completion

### Leadership

Education Leaders · Community conversation hosts · Research volunteers

### Coalition

Partner organizations · New organizations · Organization activity

### Community

Educational events · Presentations delivered · Resource downloads

### Research

Arkansas-specific resources · Local educational content · County profile completeness

Indicators visualize progress without competitive rankings.
