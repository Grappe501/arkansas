# Citizens United Knowledge Atlas & Learning Path System

**Educational Architecture v1.0** · **Build #19**  
**Dashboard:** [/mission-control/atlas.html](/mission-control/atlas.html) · **Registry:** [/data/knowledge-atlas.json](/data/knowledge-atlas.json)

---

## Purpose

The Citizens United Education Platform should not be experienced as a traditional website. It should feel like exploring a **living knowledge atlas**.

Visitors should always know:

* Where they are
* What they have learned
* What they have not explored
* How every topic connects
* What they should explore next

The Knowledge Atlas is the educational backbone of the entire platform.

---

## Core Philosophy

The platform answers one question:

> **"How did one Supreme Court decision reshape the conversation about money and politics in America?"**

Everything else supports understanding that question.

---

## The Seven Learning Worlds

Rather than presenting pages, the platform is organized into seven major learning worlds. Each world contains progressively deeper layers of exploration (**districts**).

### World One — The World Before Citizens United

Help visitors understand the decades of history leading to the case.

Districts: Early Campaign Finance · Progressive Era · Watergate · Reform Movement · Supreme Court History · McCain-Feingold · The Political Climate Before 2010

### World Two — Inside the Case

Allow visitors to experience the case from beginning to end.

Districts: The Movie · Citizens United · The Federal Election Commission · Lower Courts · Supreme Court · Oral Arguments · Majority Opinion · Dissents · Immediate Reactions

### World Three — Constitutional Foundations

Teach constitutional concepts without assuming legal training.

Districts: First Amendment · Political Speech · Corporate Speech · Associations · Judicial Review · Constitutional Interpretation · Important Precedents

### World Four — What Changed?

Document measurable consequences after 2010.

Districts: Campaign Spending · Independent Expenditures · Super PACs · Disclosure · Election Trends · State Impacts · Public Debate

### World Five — The National Conversation

Present the strongest ideas from across the public debate.

Districts: Arguments Supporting the Decision · Arguments Criticizing the Decision · Constitutional Perspectives · Public Policy Perspectives · Academic Perspectives · Frequently Misunderstood Issues

### World Six — Arkansas Solutions Center

Connect national understanding with Arkansas civic education.

Districts: Federal Legislative Options · Arkansas Legislative Options · Educational Policy Concepts · Model Law Workspace · Ballot Initiative Lab · Community Education · Coalition Building

### World Seven — Arkansas Education Network

Grow a statewide civic education movement.

Districts: Become an Education Leader · Join the Contact Network · Coalition Partners · County Education Teams · Community Conversations · Educational Events · Resource Library · Mission Control

## The Learning Compass

Every page should display a Learning Compass showing:

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| Current World | Which learning world the reader is in |
| Current District | Sub-topic within the world |
| Estimated Reading Time | Time commitment |
| Learning Level | L1–L4 depth |
| Evidence Strength | Linked fact verification status |
| Related Topics | KG and atlas connections |
| Next Recommended Destination | Guided navigation |

## Knowledge Trails

Guided educational journeys:

| Trail | Focus | Duration |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Beginner Trail** | First-time orientation | 20–30 min |
| **History Trail** | Campaign finance history before 2010 | Variable |
| **Legal Trail** | Supreme Court doctrine and reasoning | Variable |
| **Data Trail** | Political spending and measurable impacts | Variable |
| **Arkansas Trail** | Education, coalition, state civic engagement | Variable |
| **Research Trail** | Primary sources, datasets, academic materials | Variable |

      → Primary Sources
        → Educational Resources
          → Community Action Opportunities

Every completed page lights up within the atlas, making progress visible for the development team and readers.

## Educational Completion

Mission Control calculates educational readiness based on:

* Historical coverage
* Case coverage
* Constitutional coverage
* Impact coverage
* Solutions coverage
* Arkansas content
* Coalition resources
* Research completeness

This measures the **maturity of the educational institution** — not simply counting completed pages.

## Governing Principle

The Citizens United Education Platform should not feel like a website with menus.

It should feel like entering a carefully designed civic learning environment where every path leads to greater understanding, every topic connects to another, and every visitor has the opportunity to become an informed educator within their own Arkansas community.
