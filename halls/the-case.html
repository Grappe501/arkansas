Hall 2: The Case Itself

Make the court case understandable — almost like a legal documentary.

Drill-down topics

  • Who was Citizens United?
  • What was Hillary: The Movie?
  • Why did the FEC get involved?
  • What question did the Court answer?
  • Majority opinion
  • Dissent
  • What the ruling did and did not say

Citizens United is a conservative nonprofit that made a film critical of Hillary Clinton during the 2008 election. The FEC said it violated rules on corporate electioneering. The Supreme Court broadened the case to ask whether the government could ban corporate independent political spending at all. The Court said no — that such spending is protected speech.
