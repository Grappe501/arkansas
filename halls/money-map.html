Hall 4: The Money Map

Follow the dollars — from individual donor to election ad.

Money flows through multiple channels: direct contributions (limited), PACs, Super PACs (unlimited independent spending), and nonprofits that may not disclose donors. Citizens United opened corporate and union independent spending; other rules still limit direct gifts to candidates. Coordination and disclosure remain central battlegrounds.
