Hall 3: What Changed After 2010

Show the measurable impact — not theory, but what shifted in American elections.

After 2010, outside spending surged. Super PACs raised unlimited funds from wealthy donors and spent independently on ads. Nonprofits spent heavily with limited donor disclosure. State and local races saw more outside money. Researchers debate how much Citizens United directly caused versus other legal and political factors.
