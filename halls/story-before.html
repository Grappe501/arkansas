Hall 1: The Story Before Citizens United

Show that this did not come from nowhere. Decades of law, scandal, and reform led to January 2010.

Drill-down topics

  • 1907 Tillman Act
  • Watergate
  • Buckley v. Valeo
  • PACs
  • McCain-Feingold
  • The corporate money question before 2010

Congress began restricting corporate political spending over a century ago. Watergate exposed secret money and led to major reforms. Buckley v. Valeo (1976) held that spending money on politics is speech — but allowed contribution limits. PACs, soft money, and McCain-Feingold followed. By 2010, the corporate independent-expenditure question was ripe for the Supreme Court.
