Who Can Act?

Distinguish proposals by the level of authority required:

  • Federal — Congress, FEC, federal statutes
  • State — Legislatures, ballot initiatives, state ethics bodies
  • Constitutional amendment — Article V process
  • Private-sector — Shareholder governance, institutional policy
  • Community / civic — Education, forums, local leadership
  • Litigation — Court challenges testing precedent
